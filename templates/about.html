About Green2B

Green2B is a B2B marketplace built to simplify the sourcing of sustainable and eco-friendly products. Our platform connects businesses with verified green suppliers, making it easier to find and purchase products that align with environmental values.

We support companies in meeting their ESG goals by simplifying procurement through:

  • Impact Transparency: Our AI-driven sustainability scoring provides clear insights into the environmental impact of products.
  • Bulk Order Aggregation: We help businesses save costs and reduce waste by aggregating bulk orders.
  • Verified Green Suppliers: Our platform features a curated list of suppliers who meet strict sustainability criteria.
  • AI Sustainability Scoring: We use AI to evaluate and score products based on their environmental impact, helping businesses make informed decisions.

Today's Problems

  • Limited Access to Sustainable Suppliers: Businesses struggle to find reliable suppliers who are environmentally responsible for sustainable products, leading to inefficiencies and missed opportunities.
  • Unstable Demand Faced By Suppliers: Inconsistent demand for eco-friendly materials causes 30-40% operational inefficiencies
  • High Costs: Sustainable products often come with higher costs, which can deter businesses from making eco-friendly choices. High costs deter 45%+ of businesses from using sustainable alternatives
  • Difficult to verify suppliers: Verifying suppliers' sustainability claims complicate tracking the environmental impact of purchases.
  • No centralized marketplace: The lack of a centralized platform for sustainable products makes it challenging for businesses to find and compare options, increasing transaction costs and inefficiencies.

Our Solutions

  • Quality Assurance: Green2B partners only with B Corp, Fairtrade, or certified suppliers, requiring strict product testing for sustainability and ethical sourcing.
  • Environmental Impact Transparency: Each product on Green2B is evaluated and given a Sustainability Score. Real-time dashboards to compare metrics across products and suppliers
  • Logistics Simplification: Green2B handles delivery and logistics, allowing suppliers to focus on production.
  • Diverse Supplier Network Offering: Green2B offers a broad range of suppliers, providing customisation services to SMEs with competitive pricing and quality choices for eco-friendly products
  • Economies of Scale: Green2B enables suppliers to reduce costs through increased production capacity and efficiencies in raw materials, logistics, and distribution
