About Green2B

Your trusted B2B marketplace for sustainable sourcing. We simplify bulk procurement of eco-friendly products by connecting you with certified green suppliers.

Today's Challenges

  • Limited Access to Sustainable Suppliers: Businesses struggle to find verified, eco-conscious suppliers.
  • Unstable Demand for Suppliers: Inconsistent demand causes 30–40% inefficiencies.
  • High Costs: 45%+ of businesses avoid green options due to pricing barriers.
  • Lack of Verification: It’s difficult to trust unverified sustainability claims.
  • No Central Marketplace: Fragmented sources increase time, costs, and complexity.

Our Solutions

  • Verified Suppliers Only: B Corp, Fairtrade, or certified partners only—no greenwashing.
  • Sustainability Score: Every product is rated and trackable through impact dashboards.
  • Logistics Covered: Green2B handles all delivery and fulfillment logistics.
  • Bulk Aggregation for SMEs: Better pricing, lower MOQs, and customization available.
  • Scale with Impact: Support supplier growth while reducing your cost per unit.

Businesses / Buyers

Green2B streamlines your path to sustainability by making procurement simple, affordable, and verifiable.

Suppliers

Green2B helps scale your reach and improves profitability through aggregated orders and logistics support.

The Environment

  • SDG 12: Target 20% waste reduction in hospitality & retail.
  • SDG 13: Estimated 25–40% carbon footprint reduction. We partner with DHL GoGreen Plus to cut delivery emissions by up to 80%.
  • SDG 8 & 17: Cross-sector collaboration to stabilize green supply chains and promote inclusive economic growth.
